About TruthScan

Fighting Misinformation with AI

Advanced multi-agent architecture designed to combat the spread of false information

The Challenge

In today's digital landscape, misinformation spreads 6x faster than factual news. Traditional fact-checking methods are too slow and limited in scope to address this growing threat to informed decision-making.

Our Solution

TruthScan employs a sophisticated multi-agent AI system that processes claims through multiple specialized agents, each contributing unique analytical capabilities to provide comprehensive verification results.
